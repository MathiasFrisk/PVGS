DISCORD RULES

The rules below are expected to be followed at all times and may change at any time.
Staff members have the rights to use common sense and punish for offenses not listed below.



Rule #1 - Racism
Prejudice, discrimination, or antagonism directed against a person or people on the basis of their membership of a particular racial or ethnic group, typically one that is a minority or marginalized is strongly prohibited. This will not be tolerated and will cause an instant punishment.

Rule #2 - Spam
Sending more than 3 messages within a short time period is considered spam. We do not allow this, and we will warn you as fast as humanly possible, and if such behavior continues you will be muted.

Rule #3 - Advertisement
Sending links to external services/websites without permission from a staff member is not allowed. a notice or announcement in a public medium promoting a product, service, or event or publicizing a job vacancy is prohibited. You will not be warned if you break this rule. You will be muted as fast as humanly possible.

Rule #4 - Profanity
Any type of profanity, blasphemous or obscene language is prohibited. You will be warned by a staff member as fast as humanly possible, and if such behavior continues you will be muted.

Rule #5 - Channel Topics
Follow channel topics at all times. Every message you send should fit the topic of the channel you are sending it in. You will be warned if you don't follow this rule, and muted if you continue to break it.

Rule #6 - Toxicity
Any type of toxicity towards other users is strongly prohibited. You will not be warned, but instantly muted if you break this rule.

Rule #7 - Arguments
We do not want to see arguments occouring on our Discord server. If you're arguing, take it to private messages. You will be warned if you break this rule, and muted if such behavior continues.

Rule #8 - Common Sense
Use common sense. Common sense is good sense and sound judgement in practical matters. You know what is right and what is wrong. If you break this rule, you will be punished.
